Maqam · مقام
▾

Maqam isn't just a scale — it's a melodic personality. Where Western theory defines static note sets, maqam prescribes movement: how you ascend, descend, ornament, and resolve. Each maqam carries centuries of emotional meaning.

⚠ These are 12-tone approximations. True maqam uses quarter tones and microtonal inflections that live between piano keys.
