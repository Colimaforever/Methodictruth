Free Online Song Analyzer & Chord Finder

Instant Music Analysis from YouTube

Our song analyzer tool lets you discover the musical DNA of any track on YouTube. Simply paste a YouTube link and get instant analysis of chords, key signature, tempo (BPM), and song structure — then play the video back with the current chord highlighted in real time. Whether you're learning to play a song, transcribing music, or studying music theory, this free chord analyzer makes it easy.

Features

  • Chord Detection: Automatic chord recognition with timestamps throughout the song
  • Synced Play-Along: Watch the current chord light up as the song plays, like Chordify
  • Key Finder: Identifies the musical key (major or minor) of any track
  • BPM Analysis: Detects tempo and beats per minute accurately
  • YouTube Integration: Works directly with YouTube links — no downloads needed
  • Free & Unlimited: No registration, no limits, completely free to use

How to Use

  • Find a song on YouTube you want to analyze
  • Copy the YouTube URL from your browser
  • Paste the link into the analyzer above and click "Analyze"
  • Hit play and follow along as the current chord lights up
  • Click any chord in the timeline to jump straight to that moment

Note: analysis accuracy depends on audio quality and song complexity. Best results with clear recordings and standard Western music theory chord progressions.
