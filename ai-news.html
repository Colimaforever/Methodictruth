𒀭 Staying Current Without Drowning

AI moves fast enough that "staying up to date" can turn into a part-time job. This feed exists to compress that down: research (arXiv preprints, lab blogs) tells you what's coming; industry & product (company announcements, releases) tells you what just shipped; media & analysis (independent tech press) tells you what it actually means and who's pushing back.

Use the search box to track a specific thread — a model name, a company, a technique — across every source at once. Toggle sources off if one publication is drowning out the rest; your preferences are remembered. Click a category tab to switch between "what's new" and "what's true."

Dispatches are cached for 20 minutes to keep things fast and avoid hammering the feeds — hit Refresh if you want the bleeding edge right now.
